Smart Travel Guide

Actionable tips, checklists, and safety guides customized for your forecasted delay risk.

Low Delay Risk Plan

Suggested protocol for flights with less than 20% delay probability.

  • Standard Departure: Arrive 2 hours prior to flight time.
  • Web Check-in: Pre-select your boarding pass and drop luggage early.
  • Lounge Access: No need to budget excessive lounge wait times.
  • Connections: Tight layovers (45-60 mins) are generally safe.

Moderate Delay Risk Plan

Action plan for flights with 20% - 50% delay probability.

  • Monitor Status: Check airline app every 30 mins before leaving for the airport.
  • Carry-on Preferred: Travel with carry-on only if possible to preserve flexibility if re-routing is required.
  • Power Backup: Charge all electronic devices and bring a power bank.
  • Backup Connections: Double check options for later flights in case you miss a connection.

High Delay Risk Protocol

Essential steps for flights with greater than 50% delay probability.

  • Alternative Flights: Bookmark alternative departures by the same carrier.
  • Travel Insurance: Have your policy details handy for meal/hotel compensation if delays exceed 4 hours.
  • Comfort kit: Pack a light jacket, snacks, and any essential medications in your hand baggage.
  • Lounge Access: If eligible, plan to wait in a lounge as gate delays could be prolonged.
